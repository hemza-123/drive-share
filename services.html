Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our services

How does the insurance work?
All DriveShare rentals include comprehensive insurance coverage. For drivers, this means you're protected against damage and liability during your rental period. For car owners, your vehicle is fully insured while being used by renters, and any claims won't affect your personal insurance policy.
What if I need to extend my rental?
Extending your rental is easy! Simply open the DriveShare app, navigate to your current rental, and select "Extend Rental." If the car is available for the additional time, your reservation will be extended instantly. If not, our system will help you find alternative vehicles nearby.
How do I list my car and how much can I earn?
Listing your car takes less than 10 minutes through our app or website. You'll need to provide photos, details about your vehicle, and set your availability. Earnings vary based on your car's make, model, year, and location, but owners in major Algerian cities typically earn between 20,000-60,000 DA monthly when sharing their car regularly.
Are there any age restrictions for drivers?
Yes, drivers must be at least 21 years old to use DriveShare. For luxury vehicles and larger SUVs, drivers must be 25 or older. Drivers under 25 may be subject to a young driver surcharge depending on the vehicle category.
What cities do you operate in?
DriveShare currently operates in Algiers, Oran, Constantine, Annaba, Setif, Blida, and Tlemcen. We're rapidly expanding to more cities across Algeria, with plans to be in all major urban areas by the end of 2025.
