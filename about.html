Our Story

From a simple idea to Algeria's first peer-to-peer car sharing platform

Founded in 2025, DriveShare emerged from a simple observation: thousands of cars sit idle across Algeria while many people struggle to find affordable transportation options.

Our founders, a group of inelectronics student club members and transportation experts, set out to create a solution that would benefit both car owners and those needing temporary wheels. What began as a small operation in Algiers has now expanded to serve communities across the country.

Today, DriveShare connects thousands of car owners with reliable drivers, creating a trusted community marketplace that turns vehicles from expenses into income-generating assets.

Our Mission

Creating sustainable transportation solutions for all Algerians

At DriveShare, we believe in creating a more efficient transportation ecosystem that benefits everyone. Our mission is to reduce the total number of cars on the road while making mobility more accessible and affordable.

We're committed to supporting local communities by keeping transportation money within neighborhoods and reducing the environmental impact of private vehicle ownership. By utilizing existing resources more efficiently, we're building a more sustainable future.

Looking ahead, we aim to integrate electric vehicles into our platform and collaborate with public transportation systems to create a comprehensive, eco-friendly mobility network throughout Algeria.
